PRIVATE RESIDENCEKITCHENER ONTARIO
This is a compact three bedroom home for a family of four. Its massing results from the development of a simple T plan with cross gables. The style of this building reflects the vernacular of simple frame farm houses that are ubiquitous across North America and which are generally classified as Folk style. Its salient features include high ceiling porches, steep roofs, vertically proportioned windows and white on white frame cladding. An economical building, the materials and construction conform to mass production home building methods. There is an orderly simplicity to this house and it functions very practically as a single family home.
Status: Completed 1997 / Addition 2003
Project Type: Custom Residence
Design Field: Architecture / Interiors / Landscape
Building Size: 2,000 sq ft approx
Location: Kitchener ON
