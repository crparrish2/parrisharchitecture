PARK PAVILIONVILLAGE BY THE ARBORETUM
This small pavilion is constructed on a roundabout within this respected condominium community. It serves as a rendezvous location for the local residents and as a local mail depot. Its symmetry and central location at the roundabout gives it prominence within the community and terminates vistas in several directions. Its retro-style is slightly reminiscent of a Romanesque railway station with its wide overhangs, big roof brackets and deep masonry arches. This ornate structure is an integral part of the picturesque landscape of the Village.
Status: Constructed 1997
Project Type: Landscape Pavilion
Design Field: Architecture / Urban Design
Building Size: 300 sq ft approx
Location: Guelph ON
