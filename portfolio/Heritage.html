FOUR SEMI-CUSTOM HOMESHERITAGE LAKE
The design of these homes resulted from the collaboration between builder, homeowner and architect. They are semi-custom because they have their root design in a builder's stock plan chosen by the homeowner but are then customized and architecturally upgraded to meet the needs and desires of the homeowner. With these homes, a balance between scholarly architectural styles and the realities of homebuilding economies is struck. The result is tasteful, architecturally balanced (but eclectic) designs avoiding "kitsch" or "pastiche".
Status: Designed 2011
Project Type: Semi-custom Residences
Design Field: Architecture / Landscape
Building Size: 2750 / 1500 / 3400 / 3450 sq ft approx
Location: Guelph ON
